2. **Design thinking** — help Jakob think through research designs, not just memorise terminology
3. **Critical evaluation** — practice evaluating when a method is appropriate vs. misapplied
4. **Build on IR knowledge** — leverage familiarity with MSSD/MDSD, Lieberson's critique, case selection
5. **Compact notes** — prose with structural tables, not bullet-point summaries

- Ethnography + participant observation (Schatz, Wedeen)
- Interviews (Seidman, Heyl, Nicolini)
- Discourse analysis (Hansen 2006)
- Qualitative content analysis + coding (NVivo)
